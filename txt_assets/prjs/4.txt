I started building this site back in 2023 but shelved it when my direction was still fuzzy. In early 2026, after clearing Security+ and figuring out exactly where I want to land in security operations, I came back to it with actual purpose.
<br><br>
The design is intentionally not another beige minimalist template. Everyone has those. I wanted something that actually looks like me, where you can get a sense of who I am as you browse through. That said, I kept the structure ruthless: employers can find my work and contact details in seconds without digging through my life story.
